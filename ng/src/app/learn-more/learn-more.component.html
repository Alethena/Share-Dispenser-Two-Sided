How It Works

Please read through the steps below carefully and do not hesitate to contact us at contact@alethena.com if you are unsure about how to proceed.

Alethena's solution includes a smart contract on the Ethereum blockchain, a legal setup in collaboration with LEXR, and a digital share register from Ledgy which syncs from the blockchain automatically.

On this page you can purchase (and sell) real world Alethena shares directly from our share dispenser and then register as a shareholder. Registration is not mandatory, but necessary in order to exercise full shareholder rights. To ensure a stable CHF/ETH exchange rate, payments are to be made in Crypto Francs (XCHF), which serves as a digital Swiss Franc and is structured as a bond.

Buy and Sell Your Shares

1
Assuming that you have a sufficient amount of XCHF on your account, select the number of shares you would like to buy (or sell).
The minimum amount is fixed at 20 shares per trade. When you are ready, click «Buy Now» (or «Sell Now»).
2
A pop-up will open, prompting you to acknowledge the terms set forth in the Prospectus, Share Token Terms, and the Alethena Privacy Policy. Click «Agree» and, after carefully reading the process description that appears, click «Proceed».
3
Confirm the first MetaMask pop-up which handles the payment authorization.
Important: Transactions on the blockchain can take a while to process. Please do not refresh the window.
4
After the first transaction went through, please confirm the second MetaMask pop-up which handles the actual share purchase (or share sale). Again, please be patient and do not refresh the window.
5
On the main network, there is an additional step which consits of registring in the shareholder registry at Ledgy. This step needs to be completed in order to exercise full shareholder rights. At the moment, this is not available in the test environment.
