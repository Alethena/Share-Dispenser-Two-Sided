Fully tokenized equity on the Ethereum blockchain

Please read through the steps below carefully and do not hesitate to contact us at contact@alethena.com if you are unsure about how to proceed.

Buying shares from the share dispenser is not a complex process, please find the necessary steps below.



Payments should be made in Crypto Francs (XCHF). Therefore the first step is to obtain a sufficient amount of XCHF for your purchase. There are a number of ways to get XCHF, you can find them here.
Assuming that you have a sufficient amount of XCHF on your account, log into MetaMask and select the account you use to hold the XCHF. You should see your XCHF balance in the middle of the box on the right of the screen.
Select the number of Alethena shares you would like to buy and click "Buy now for...". A pop-up from MetaMask will open asking you to authorize the XCHF payment. Click "Confirm" and wait until the transaction has completed. At this point the trade is not yet complete, this is due to an idiosyncrasy of the ERC-20 standard.
A second pop-up from MetaMask will open — this is the actual purchase of Alethena Shares.
Click confirm again and wait for the transaction to finish.
You are now a shareholder of Alethena. Welcome and get in touch!
