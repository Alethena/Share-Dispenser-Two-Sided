Your Guide to Alethena Shares

Please read through the steps below carefully and do not hesitate to contact us at contact@alethena.com if you are unsure about how to proceed. For a visual walk-through please watch our video presentation.

Get Ready!

To buy shares you need MetaMask, Crypto Francs (XCHF) as well as a small amount of Ether. Read our Medium Post to learn how to do this.

Buy Your Shares

1
Assuming that you have a sufficient amount of XCHF on your account, select the number of shares you would like to buy.
The minimum amount is fixed at 20 shares per trade. When you are ready, click “Buy Now”.
2
A pop-up will open, prompting you to acknowledge the terms set forth in the Prospectus, Share Token Terms, and the Alethena Privacy Policy. Click «Agree» and, after carefully reading the process description that appears, click «Proceed».
3
Confirm the first MetaMask pop-up which handles the payment authorization.
Important: Transactions on the blockchain can take a while to process. Please do not refresh the window.
4
After the first transaction went through, please confirm the second MetaMask pop-up which handles the actual share purchase. Again, please be patient and do not refresh the window.

Become an Active Alethena Shareholder!

After finalizing the second transaction, please register in our shareholder registry at Ledgy to become a shareholder with executable rights.
