Alethena Share Dispenser

Explore a truly digital way to become a shareholder

The Alethena Share Dispenser has two sides: The first side constitutes an offer to the Swiss public to buy up to 2'000 tokenized registered shares of Equility AG for between CHF 6.00 and CHF 6.50 each (the price increases linearly with each sold share), raising up to CHF 12'500 at an implied market capitalization of up to CHF 9 million. The second side is an offer to buy shares back from Swiss shareholders.

These shares will be held on the balance sheet of Equility AG. The main purpose of these offers is to demonstrate and test our share dispenser. These offers are for Swiss residents only , for other jurisdictions please check your local requirements.

Please note that these offers are non-binding and in particular subject to the availability of share tokens or payment tokens as well as other requirements Equility AG may impose from time to time. Equility AG reserves the right to change the terms of or revoke these offers at any time.

You can read more about the share purchase offer in our prospectus and under investors relations. Note that you can also request an offer through traditional means. If you wish to do so, please get in touch by e-mail.

To buy and sell shares you need MetaMask, Crypto Francs (XCHF) as well as a small amount of Ether. Read our Medium Post to learn how to do this.
