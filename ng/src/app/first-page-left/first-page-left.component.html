Alethena Share Dispenser

Explore a truly digital way to become a shareholder

Alethena has developed a tokenized equity framework and successfully applied it to the entire share capital of its underlying company Equility AG.

The solution includes a smart contract on the Ethereum blockchain, a legal setup in collaboration with LEXR, and a digital share register from Ledgy which syncs from the blockchain automatically.

On this page you can purchase shares directly from our share dispenser and then register as a shareholder. Registration is not mandatory, but necessary in order to exercise full shareholder rights.

To ensure a stable CHF/ETH exchange rate, payments are to be made in Crypto Francs (XCHF), which serves as a digital Swiss Franc and is structured as a bond.
